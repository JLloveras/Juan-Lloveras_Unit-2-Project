Our Purpose

Our purpose as a website is to inform as many people as possible about the lack of a national paid leave policy and about the statistics of overall job satisfcation, paid leave mandates, and more. We aim to make paid leave a mandatory benefit for everyone, increasing job satisfaction and overall quality of life. We hope to make family vacation, bonding, and health a more achievable thought in everybody's life.
